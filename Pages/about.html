Who We Are

The National Land & Disaster Management Authority (NLDMA) is the result of a visionary merger between two pivotal institutions:

NDMA

Pakistan's federal authority for disaster risk management

LIMS

A geospatial intelligence initiative supporting land, agriculture, and environmental monitoring

This merger brings together emergency preparedness, land use data, and GIS technology under one national platform for comprehensive resilience building.

Our Mission

"To harness land intelligence and disaster management for the safety, sustainability, and prosperity of Pakistan."

Our Vision

A climate-resilient Pakistan where data, technology, and coordination protect every life, livelihood, and landscape.
